Who we are

Alfaleads Group is an international company that includes the affiliate program Alfaleads Network, the search marketing agency Alfa: search, the influencer marketing company AAA Agency and the international igaming event BIG conference.

Brands

Alfaleads Network

An international Affiliate Marketing agency with expertise in iGaming and Betting. Exclusive offers and comprehensive support for affiliates. A slew of traffic sources, consistent volumes and high quality traffic for advertisers

Alfa: search

A fast-moving global challenger, focused on developing and providing innovative B2B solutions, aimed at maximizing ROI and product visibility through strategic application of Search Engine Optimization and App Search Optimization

AAA Agency

An influence and special projects agency dedicated to transforming marketing campaigns into unforgettable experiences. It creates authentic brand stories through collabs with influencers, and builds audience trust with the clients

Big Conference

The Balkan iGaming Conference is a new affiliate marketing conference organized by Alfaleads Group in Belgrade, Serbia on June 12th and 13th, 2025. The event will bring together the top players of the iGaming industry
